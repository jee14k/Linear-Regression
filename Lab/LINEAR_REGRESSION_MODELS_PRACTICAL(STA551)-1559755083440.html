Course Description and Course Objectives:

The course is designed to provide a practical exposure to the students in Simple and Multiple linear Regression Analysis.

Course Outcomes

CO1: The students are able to develop a deeper understanding of the linear regression model by visualizing and fitting models with minimum error using R programming.

CO2:The students are able to understand the concept of hetroskedasticity and multicollinearity with respect to real life problems.

Unit-1Teaching Hours:30

List of Practical Assignments

1. Scatter Plots diagnosis.

2. Estimation of simple regression model.

3. Significance of simple linear regression.

4. Confidence Interval Estimation of simple linear regression.

5. Estimation of Multiple regression model.

6. Significance and confidence interval for multiple linear Regression.

7. Residuals Plots, detection of outliers and their interpretation in simple and multiple linear regression.

8. Checking for Normality of Residuals.

9. Checking for Multicollinearity in simple and multiple linear regression.

Checking for Heteroscedasticity and auto-correlation in simple and multiple linear regression.
